About Me

Oritsemajemite (MJ) Ukpohor is from Delta State, Nigeria. She has been a family lifestyle photographer for 4 years. She's currently a 1st level student at Algonquin College, Ottawa, Canada where she’s studying Interactive Media Design. MJ also volunteers as a life coach for teenagers and young adults at the Youth Resource Center, Bonny Island, Nigeria.

Her favorite quote is:
It's only when you have stepped outside your comfort zone that you truly begin to change, grow and transform. Roy T Bennet.

MJ enjoys having a nice hot meal alongside good conversations with close friends and strangers alike. A wife of 1 husband, and a mother of 4 children, MJ's family is her greatest treasure.

Her hobbies include

  • watching documentary dramas
  • singing/listening to good music.
